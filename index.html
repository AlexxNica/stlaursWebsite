The STLAURS Planning Committee is proud to present the 2012 St. Louis Area Undergraduate Research Symposium, an annual event held each spring that allows hundreds of undergraduates from outstanding St. Louis area universities the opportunity to present their research or creative project results in a professional setting. Any undergraduate student from these institutions who has conducted research or a creative project with a faculty mentor is invited to submit an application and abstract.

STLAURS provides an excellent opportunity for students to learn about the research and creative projects of their peers, speak to the attending university faculty, interact with industry representatives, and receive both wisdom and inspiration from the invited speakers. Members of the larger St. Louis and Midwest scientific research community are invited to attend. This unique event is a collaboration involving Southern Illinois University Carbondale, Southern Illinois University Edwardsville, St. Louis University, University of Missouri at St. Louis, and Washington University in St. Louis.
