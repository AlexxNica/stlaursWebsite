Presentation Format
Poster Presentation
Verbal Presentation

Students have the option of submitting their application as an oral presentation, and a small number will be invited to do so. Those who are not invited to give an oral presentation will automatically be considered applicants for a poster presentation. Oral presentations should be 8-10 minutes in length with five additional minutes for a question-and-answer period. PowerPoint may be used. Speakers who are confirmed for their presentation should make arrangements with Elisabeth Jones to make sure their presentation formats will work. Speakers are highly encouraged to arrive early to the event to confirm that all A/V materials are compatible and working for their specific presentation. Laptops and projectors will be provided.

Due to space limitations, only a limited number of students requesting to give oral talks will be invited to do so. Presentations will be chosen based on the quality of the abstract and research, and on variety from the perspective of the entire event and highlighting work from each participating institution.
